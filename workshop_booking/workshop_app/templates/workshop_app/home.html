About FOSSEE

FOSSEE (Free/Libre and Open Source Software for Education) is an initiative by IIT Bombay, promoting the use of FLOSS tools in education, research, and industry.

We conduct workshops, create educational content, and foster collaboration among learners, educators, and developers across the globe. By joining FOSSEE Workshops, you become part of a global movement for accessible and affordable education.
